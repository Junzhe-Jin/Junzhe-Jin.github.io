👋 Hi! I am a Master's student in Computer Science at Aarhus University, where I am affiliated with the Interactive Matter Lab (supervised by Dr. Michael Wessely). Currently, I am also conducting my Master's thesis research at the Diverse-ability Interaction Lab (Bristol Interaction Group) at the University of Bristol (supervised by Dr. Oussama Metatla).

Prior to this, I received my Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering from Northwestern Polytechnical University.

My research lies at the intersection of human-computer interaction, tangible user interfaces, human-robot interaction, and inclusive design. I am always open to new connections and potential collabrations, feel free to drop me an email!
