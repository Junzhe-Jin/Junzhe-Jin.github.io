BIOral: Modular Intraoral Interfaces

Continuous Saliva Biomarker Sensing and On-Demand Drug Delivery.

ACM Link Watch Video

Abstract

Saliva contains crucial physiological biomarkers, yet continuous and unobtrusive monitoring remains a challenge. Existing intraoral interfaces primarily focus on physical interactions rather than biochemical sensing. We introduce BIOral, a modular intraoral bio-interactive interface designed to bridge this gap. By integrating stimuli-responsive biopolymers with a custom sensing pipeline, BIOral enables both continuous salivary pH monitoring and programmable on-demand drug delivery.

Hardware & CapCell Design

The hardware architecture relies on a custom SLA 3D-printed dental retainer made from biocompatible Dental SG resin. To ensure safety and adaptability, we developed CapCell, a modular capsule system with a threaded locking mechanism. This allows users to easily swap sensing or drug-release modules without replacing the entire retainer. The core electronics include an ultra-small nRF52840 microcontroller and a digital color sensor (TCS34725) securely encapsulated using food-grade epoxy.

Colorimetric Sensing Pipeline

For pH sensing, we fabricated edible hydrogels by combining anthocyanin and κ-carrageenan. As the saliva pH changes, the hydrogel changes color. BIOral's embedded RGB sensor captures these colorimetric shifts. We implemented a robust signal-processing algorithm that normalizes the RGB readings and maps them to highly accurate pH estimates (ranging from pH 2 to 10), overcoming challenges like oral cavity darkness and sensor variations.

Programmable Drug Release

Beyond sensing, BIOral acts as an active therapeutic device. We engineered vitamin-infused hydrogels housed within the CapCell. By parametrically tuning the geometric aperture of the CapCell during the 3D printing process, we achieved programmable, sustained intraoral drug diffusion. In-vitro evaluations demonstrated predictable release durations ranging from 1 to 3.75 hours, proving its efficacy for clinical dental applications.
